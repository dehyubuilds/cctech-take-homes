# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Twilly is committed to providing a fair and transparent refund process for our users. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted and the procedures for requesting them.

## 2. General Refund Principles

### 2.1 Our Commitment
- We provide refunds in appropriate circumstances
- We process refund requests promptly and fairly
- We communicate clearly about refund decisions
- We maintain records of all refund transactions

### 2.2 Refund Eligibility
Refunds may be granted for:
- Technical issues preventing content access
- Billing errors or unauthorized charges
- Content that violates our policies
- Service interruptions or platform failures
- Other circumstances as determined by Twilly

### 2.3 Refund Methods
- **Original Payment Method**: Refunds processed to original payment source
- **Processing Time**: 3-10 business days depending on payment method
- **Notification**: Email confirmation when refund is processed
- **Tracking**: Refund status available in your account

## 3. Subscription Refunds

### 3.1 Monthly Subscriptions
- **Cancellation**: You may cancel at any time
- **Pro-Rated Refunds**: Available for unused portion of billing period
- **Effective Date**: Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- **No Refunds**: For partial months after cancellation

### 3.2 Annual Subscriptions
- **Pro-Rated Refunds**: Available for unused months
- **Calculation**: Based on monthly equivalent rate
- **Processing**: Refund processed within 5-7 business days
- **Notification**: Email confirmation of refund amount

### 3.3 Subscription Cancellation
To cancel a subscription:
1. Go to your account settings
2. Select "Subscriptions"
3. Choose the subscription to cancel
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Receive confirmation email

### 3.4 Cancellation Timeline
- **Immediate**: Access continues until end of billing period
- **No Further Charges**: No additional charges after cancellation
- **Content Access**: Maintain access until period ends
- **Auto-Renewal**: Disabled upon cancellation

## 4. Pay-Per-View (PPV) Refunds

### 4.1 Technical Issues
Refunds granted for:
- **Content Not Accessible**: Technical problems preventing viewing
- **Poor Quality**: Severely degraded video/audio quality
- **Platform Errors**: System failures preventing access
- **Billing Errors**: Incorrect charges or duplicate payments

### 4.2 Content Issues
Refunds may be granted for:
- **Misleading Content**: Content doesn't match description
- **Policy Violations**: Content removed for policy violations
- **Incomplete Content**: Significantly shorter than advertised
- **Quality Issues**: Unacceptable technical quality

### 4.3 Refund Timeline
- **Request Period**: 7 days from purchase date
- **Review Time**: 2-3 business days for decision
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days for refund
- **Notification**: Email confirmation of refund

### 4.4 Non-Refundable PPV
Refunds typically not granted for:
- **Change of Mind**: Simply deciding not to watch
- **Spoilers**: Content revealed in previews or descriptions
- **Personal Dislike**: Not enjoying the content
- **Technical Limitations**: User's device or connection issues

## 5. Tipping and Donation Refunds

### 5.1 Tip Refunds
- **Accidental Tips**: Refunded if clearly accidental
- **Fraudulent Tips**: Refunded if unauthorized
- **Processing Errors**: Refunded for system errors
- **Time Limit**: 24 hours from tip transaction

### 5.2 Donation Refunds
- **Campaign Cancellation**: Refunded if campaign is cancelled
- **Goal Not Met**: Refunded if funding goal not reached
- **Creator Issues**: Refunded if creator violates policies
- **Processing Errors**: Refunded for system errors

## 6. Requesting a Refund

### 6.1 How to Request
1. **Contact Support**: Email support@twilly.com
2. **Provide Details**: Include transaction information
3. **Explain Reason**: Clearly state reason for refund
4. **Attach Evidence**: Include screenshots or documentation
5. **Wait for Response**: Allow 2-3 business days for review

### 6.2 Required Information
Include in your refund request:
- **Transaction ID**: From your payment confirmation
- **Purchase Date**: When the transaction occurred
- **Amount**: Total amount paid
- **Reason**: Detailed explanation of refund request
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, error messages, or other proof

### 6.3 Refund Request Timeline
- **Submission**: Submit request within applicable time limits
- **Review**: 2-3 business days for initial review
- **Decision**: Email notification of decision
- **Processing**: 3-5 business days for refund processing

## 7. Refund Processing

### 7.1 Processing Timeline
- **Credit Cards**: 3-5 business days
- **Debit Cards**: 3-5 business days
- **Bank Transfers**: 5-10 business days
- **Digital Wallets**: 1-3 business days

### 7.2 Refund Amounts
- **Full Refunds**: Complete amount paid
- **Partial Refunds**: Pro-rated amounts for subscriptions
- **Processing Fees**: May be deducted in some cases
- **Currency Conversion**: Refunded in original currency

### 7.3 Refund Confirmation
- **Email Notification**: Confirmation when refund is processed
- **Account Credit**: May appear as account credit
- **Bank Statement**: May take additional time to appear
- **Support Contact**: Contact us if refund doesn't appear

## 8. Dispute Resolution

### 8.1 Disagreement with Refund Decision
If you disagree with a refund decision:
1. **Review Decision**: Carefully read the explanation
2. **Provide Additional Information**: Submit additional evidence
3. **Escalate**: Request review by senior support team
4. **Final Appeal**: Contact legal@twilly.com for final review

### 8.2 Chargeback Process
- **Last Resort**: Chargebacks should be last resort
- **Communication**: Contact us before filing chargeback
- **Evidence**: Provide evidence to support your case
- **Resolution**: Work with us to resolve disputes

### 8.3 Dispute Timeline
- **Initial Response**: Within 2 business days
- **Review Period**: 5-7 business days for complex cases
- **Final Decision**: Within 10 business days
- **Appeal Process**: Additional 5 business days for appeals

## 9. Special Circumstances

### 9.1 Platform Outages
- **Service Interruptions**: Refunds for significant outages
- **Content Loss**: Refunds for permanently lost content
- **Access Issues**: Refunds for extended access problems
- **Communication**: Proactive refunds for major issues

### 9.2 Policy Violations
- **Creator Violations**: Refunds if creator violates policies
- **Content Removal**: Refunds for removed content
- **Account Termination**: Refunds for terminated creator accounts
- **Legal Issues**: Refunds for legal compliance issues

### 9.3 Billing Errors
- **Duplicate Charges**: Immediate refunds for duplicates
- **Incorrect Amounts**: Refunds for billing errors
- **Unauthorized Charges**: Refunds for unauthorized transactions
- **System Errors**: Refunds for technical billing issues

## 10. International Refunds

### 10.1 Currency Conversion
- **Original Currency**: Refunds in original payment currency
- **Exchange Rates**: Current rates at time of refund
- **Conversion Fees**: May apply depending on payment method
- **Processing Time**: May take longer for international refunds

### 10.2 Regional Considerations
- **Local Laws**: Compliance with local refund laws
- **Tax Implications**: Tax treatment varies by country
- **Banking Systems**: Different processing times by region
- **Support Hours**: Regional support availability
