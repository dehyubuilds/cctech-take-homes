# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Twilly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our subscription-based content platform.

By using Twilly's services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
- **Profile Information**: Username, bio, profile pictures, creator information
- **Payment Information**: Stripe Connect account details, tax information, payout preferences
- **Content**: Videos, streams, episodes, messages, comments
- **Communications**: Support requests, feedback, marketing preferences

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Streaming Data**: Stream quality, viewer engagement, technical performance metrics
- **Location Data**: General location information (country/region level)
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session data, preferences, analytics information

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Stripe**: Payment processing and verification information
- **Social Media**: If you connect social media accounts
- **Analytics Providers**: Usage and performance data
- **Verification Services**: Identity verification information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Host and stream your content
- Manage creator collaborations and revenue sharing
- Provide customer support and technical assistance
- Send service-related communications

### 3.2 Platform Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Develop new features and functionality
- Optimize streaming performance and quality
- Conduct research and analytics

### 3.3 Legal and Security
- Comply with legal obligations and regulations
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Enforce our Terms of Service and policies
- Protect user safety and platform integrity

### 3.4 Marketing and Communications
- Send promotional materials (with your consent)
- Provide personalized recommendations
- Share platform updates and announcements

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 With Your Consent
We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We share information with trusted service providers who assist in:
- Payment processing (Stripe)
- Content delivery and streaming
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Customer support services
- Legal and compliance services

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by law, including:
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Government investigations
- Regulatory compliance

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

Content you make public on Twilly (videos, streams, public profiles) may be visible to other users and potentially indexed by search engines.

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure payment processing

### 5.2 Data Breach Response
In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify affected users promptly
- Report to relevant authorities as required
- Take immediate steps to contain and remediate
- Provide guidance on protective measures

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Periods
We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

### 6.2 Account Deletion
When you delete your account:
- Your profile and personal information will be removed
- Your content may be deleted or anonymized
- Payment and transaction records may be retained for legal compliance
- Some information may be retained for security and fraud prevention
